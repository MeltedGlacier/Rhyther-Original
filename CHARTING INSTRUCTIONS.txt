Step 1. Export your Song as a .WAV file (Make sure the .wav is lowercase when exported)
Step 2. Move your Song into the Music Folder in Assets.
Step 3. Open the Game and click on Create, Name the Chart the same name as the Song in the Music Folder.
Step 4. Chart it!
Step 5. Goto assets/charts/songList.txt and add your Song into the List.
Step 6. Make Background Art in 1280x720 Resolution, Put it in assets/charts/bg/(song name).gif
Step 7. Go into Freeplay and start Playing!
